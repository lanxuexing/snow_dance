# Ecosystem

SnowDance is built to be extensible. Our ecosystem includes a variety of plugins and tools designed to enhance your blogging experience.

## Official Plugins

- **snow_dance_cli**: A CLI tool to scaffold new articles.
- **snow_dance_theme**: A collection of premium themes.
